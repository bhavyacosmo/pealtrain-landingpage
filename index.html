Frequently Asked Questions

I have a busy schedule. Will this actually work?
Absolutely. We specialize in coaching busy professionals. Our workouts are 30-45 mins long, and our nutrition plans are built around simple, home-cooked Indian meals that require zero extra prep time.
I've tried everything. What makes this different?
Most programs fail because they lack accountability. Peak Train provides elite 1-on-1 human coaching. Your coach monitors your data daily and adjusts the plan in real-time based on your work stress, travel, and lifestyle.
Will I have to give up all my favorite foods?
No. We follow a "flexible dieting" approach. We teach you how to include pizza, desserts, and your favorite Indian snacks while still hitting your fat loss goals. Sustainability is our #1 priority.
Is this only for people with major weight issues?
Not at all. We help everyone—from CEOs looking to lose that last 5kg of belly fat to professionals looking to build muscle, improve their energy levels, and fix their relationship with food.
Do I need to join a gym or buy supplements?
No. Many of our clients get incredible results working out at home with zero equipment. Supplements are 100% optional; we focus on real, whole foods to drive 95% of your results.
What if I fall off track?
Life happens. That's exactly why you have a coach. Instead of waiting for "next Monday," your coach helps you recalibrate immediately without any guilt or judgment, ensuring a bad meal doesn't turn into a bad week.
